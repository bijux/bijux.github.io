# Bijux Proteomics

`bijux-proteomics` is the proteomics and discovery product surface in
the Bijux ecosystem. It shows how the same platform and software
discipline carries into scientific and laboratory-adjacent work.

</div>

## What Proteomics Reveals

- domain-oriented product thinking rather than generic platform abstraction
- software structure that can carry subject-matter complexity without losing clarity
- a public scientific computing surface that still reflects repository discipline

## Inspect Proteomics When

- the work is specifically about proteomics, discovery, or lab-facing workflows
- you want to see how engineering structure adapts to scientific product work
- the question is product-specific rather than purely platform-wide

## Why It Matters In The Portfolio

Proteomics shows that the portfolio can move from infrastructure and
delivery into domain systems where data, workflow, and subject-matter
context matter at the same time.
