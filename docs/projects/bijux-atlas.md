# Bijux Atlas

`bijux-atlas` is the delivery and operational surface for services,
datasets, APIs, and docs control-plane behavior. It is the strongest
public signal in the portfolio for data delivery, service architecture,
and operational visibility.

</div>

## What Atlas Reveals

- data and service surfaces designed as maintained product interfaces
- operational reporting, validation, and docs-quality behavior treated as part of delivery
- engineering attention to both machine-readable artifacts and human-readable operating context

## Inspect Atlas When

- the question is about API delivery, dataset publishing, or service behavior
- you are tracing docs UX checks, ops validation, or operational evidence
- you want the clearest public example of data-service engineering in the Bijux portfolio

## Why It Matters In The Portfolio

Atlas shows that the work extends beyond internal architecture into
delivery surfaces that have to be published, validated, and operated
credibly.
