# Bijux Canon

`bijux-canon` is the governed knowledge-system stack for deterministic
ingest, retrieval, reasoning, orchestration, and controlled runtime
acceptance. It is the clearest public demonstration of system
decomposition around AI and knowledge workflows.

## What Canon Reveals

- package boundaries that separate ingest, indexing, reasoning, agents, and runtime concerns cleanly
- governed AI and retrieval thinking that favors inspectability over mystery
- documentation that explains architecture as a maintained system rather than an afterthought

## Inspect Canon When

- the question is about ingest, indexing, reasoning, agents, or runtime proof
- you want to see how a knowledge system is split into accountable components
- the issue is in a governed AI workflow rather than a domain-specific product shell

## Why It Matters In The Portfolio

Canon demonstrates architectural judgment in a space where systems often
collapse into indistinct “AI platform” stories. The split itself carries
the argument.
