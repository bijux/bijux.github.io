About

bijux publishes small, sharp tools and course books. The contract is simple: explicit invariants, deterministic behavior, and failure modes you can trust. Everything should be testable, explainable, and boring to operate.

Principles

  • Determinism over convenience: same inputs, same outputs, same state.
  • Explicit contracts: validation at boundaries; no implicit magic.
  • Sharp failure modes: early, loud, and localized.
  • Verifiable behavior: executable checks, probes, and tests as first-class artifacts.
  • Operational clarity: predictable composition, minimal hidden coupling.
